Delhi Metro shuts entry, exit gates of several stations amid farmers protest, check full list

The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation on Tuesday (January 26) closed the entry and exit gates of various metro stations on the yellow, green, violet and blue lines after hundreds of farmers deviated from their pre-decided routes during the 'Kisan tractor rally' and clashed with cops.

According to report, the protesters entered Delhi defying agreement of the scheduled time for the tractor rally.
Besides this, entry and exit gates of all stations on green line have been shuttered.
Thousands of tractors rolled into Delhi even as the 72nd Republic Day parade was underway. Vandalism was reported from ITO where the Police Headquarters are situated.
